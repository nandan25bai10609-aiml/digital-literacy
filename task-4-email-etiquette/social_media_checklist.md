#  **Social Media Etiquette Checklist for College Students**

---

## ✅ **The Do’s**

* **Use privacy settings appropriately** to manage who can access your personal information and posts.
* **Develop a professional network** by connecting with industry professionals, alumni, and peers on platforms such as LinkedIn.
* **Verify the accuracy of information** before sharing to prevent the spread of misinformation.
* **Maintain respectful communication** by using clear, appropriate, and professional language in all online interactions.
* **Highlight your academic and professional achievements**, including projects, certifications, and skills, to build a positive digital presence.

---

## ❌ **The Don’ts**

* **Do not share confidential or sensitive information** related to your institution, faculty members, or peers without proper consent.
* **Avoid engaging in online conflicts or inappropriate discussions** that may harm your reputation.
* **Do not post content that is inappropriate or unprofessional**, as it may negatively impact future academic or career opportunities.
* **Avoid excessive or irrelevant use of hashtags**, which can reduce the credibility of your content.
* **Refrain from using informal language, slang, or text abbreviations** on professional platforms such as LinkedIn.

---

## 📌 **Conclusion**

 A responsible and professional online presence is essential in today’s digital environment. Practicing proper social media etiquette helps maintain credibility, enhances communication, and supports long-term academic and career growth.
