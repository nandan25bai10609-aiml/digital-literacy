🛡️ Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist

* *Stay Safe Online – Checklist for Students*

Do not click on suspicious links received via email, SMS, or WhatsApp.
Always check the sender’s email address and website URL before entering any details.
Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
Keep your apps and devices updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into banking or personal accounts.

* UPI & Financial Safety*

Never approve unknown UPI payment requests or “collect requests.”
Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money.
